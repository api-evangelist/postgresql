The CloudNativePG Community is excited to announce the immediate availability of CloudNativePG 1.30.0 ! This minor release introduces the new DatabaseRole CRD for declarative, GitOps-friendly PostgreSQL role management and a Lease-based primary election primitive for safer failover, alongside notable security and operational improvements, further cementing CloudNativePG as the leading operator for running PostgreSQL workloads on Kubernetes.
