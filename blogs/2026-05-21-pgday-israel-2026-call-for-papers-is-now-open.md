Dear PostgreSQL Community, We are pleased to announce that the Call for Papers for PGDay Israel 2026 is now open. We invite community members, users, and developers to submit proposals for talks and presentations. Whether you are working with PostgreSQL in production, contributing to the project, or exploring innovative use cases, we would be delighted to hear from you. Important dates: - Submission deadline: July 1, 2026 - Speaker notifications: August 1, 2026 Submission process: Please submit your proposal(s) by email to info@pgday.org.il Each proposal should include the following information:
- Title - Abstract (including any notes for the program committee) - Presentation language (English or Hebrew) - Speaker name(s) and affiliation(s) - A short biography for each speaker - Other conferences (past or future) where this proposal has been or will be submitted - Technical requirements (internet access, video, audio, etc.) - Any additional requirements We look forward to your submissions and to building an engaging and high-quality program together with the community. Best regards, Michael Goldberg On behalf of the PGDay Israel Organizing Team
