The schedule is now online. New this year: Birds of a Feather (BoF). Registration is moving quickly. Swiss PGDay 2026 The Swiss PostgreSQL Conference. 25-26 June 2026 · Rapperswil, Switzerland · Near Zurich Website The Swiss PGDay 2026 schedule is live at 2026.pgday.ch/schedule/ . Review two days of PostgreSQL sessions in English and German and plan your time in Rapperswil. New this year, Birds of a Feather (BoF) adds a discussion-led format alongside the Poster Exhibition and Lightning Talks, creating more room for practical exchange across the PostgreSQL community. Registration Registration is moving quickly Interest is strong and venue capacity is limited. Based on current demand, Swiss PGDay 2026 is likely to sell out before the conference. If you plan to join us in Rapperswil, register now to secure your place. Register for Swiss PGDay 2026 → Take Part in Swiss PGDay 2026 Birds of a Feather (BoF) — Topics due 18 June 2026 : New this year: moderated discussions around shared PostgreSQL questions, practices, and real-world work. Propose a topic via info@pgday.ch . Details. Poster Exhibition — Topics due 8 June 2026 : Posters are welcome for research, extensions, tools, blog posts, and practical PostgreSQL use cases. Accepted posters will be displayed in the exhibition area and discussed during the extended coffee and poster break. Commercial advertisements and company or product presentations are not allowed. Details. Lightning Talks — Short talks on day two : Lightning Talks last no more than 5 minutes. Sign-up on the board during the conference remains the default path, and advance proposals can also be sent to lt@pgday.ch up to one week before the event to help new contributors prepare. Details.
