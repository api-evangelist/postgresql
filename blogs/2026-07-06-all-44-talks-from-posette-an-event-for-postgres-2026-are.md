POSETTE: An Event for Postgres 2026 , took place from 16–18 June 2026 as a free and virtual event, organized by Microsoft in partnership with AMD. Although the event has concluded, all the content remains available online. All 44 talks are now on YouTube Whether you missed the conference or want to revisit your favorite sessions, all 44 talks are now available on YouTube, with captions that have been translated into 16 different languages (because accessibility matters).
