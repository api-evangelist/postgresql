The Call for Papers for the PGConf.EU 2026 main conference track, as well as the Calls for Presentations for sessions taking place during Community Events Day, are now open. All submission deadlines close on 1 June 2026 . Main Conference Track The main conference CFP can be found here . The conference will feature 45-minute and 25-minute sessions, all held in English. We welcome presentations on any PostgreSQL-related topic. For topic suggestions, please see the conference website. You can also review last year’s schedule for inspiration. Community Events Day Community Events Day will feature a number of community-led sessions and events taking place on Friday, 23 October 2026. Descriptions of the individual events and their focus areas can be found here . If you are interested in contributing to any of the Community Events Day sessions, please review the topics and submit your presentation proposal via the submission link available on each individual event page. We look forward to welcoming another diverse range of PostgreSQL community discussions, experiences, and ideas at PGConf.EU 2026.
